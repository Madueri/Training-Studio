Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MAD Training Studio.

What is MAD Training Studio? +

MAD Training Studio is an AI-powered training platform for professional interpreters. It offers realistic scenarios across 7 interpreting modes, with real-time feedback, progress tracking, and certification-aligned content.

Which interpreting modes are supported? +

We support Shadowing, Consecutive, Simultaneous, Chuchotage (Whispered), Sight Translation, OPI (Over-the-Phone), and Legal Interpreting.

Is it suitable for beginners? +

Absolutely. Our Beginner mode starts with Shadowing and Sight Translation, gradually building skills toward more advanced modes like Simultaneous and Legal.

Can I use it to prepare for certification exams? +

Yes. Our scenarios and rubrics are aligned with AIIC, CCHI, NBCMI, and NAJIT standards. Pro users get access to dedicated certification prep tracks.

What language pairs are available? +

Arabic-English is our most complete pair. We also support Arabic-French, English-French, and multiple dialects including Levantine, Gulf, Egyptian, and Maghrebi.

How does the AI feedback work? +

After each session, our AI analyzes your interpretation for accuracy, completeness, terminology usage, and delivery quality. You receive a detailed breakdown with specific improvement suggestions.

Is there a free trial? +

Yes. The Basic plan is free forever. Pro plans come with a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time. You will retain access until the end of your billing period.

Do you offer team or institutional licenses? +

Yes. Our Enterprise plan includes team management, custom scenario creation, LMS integration, and dedicated support. Contact us for a custom quote.

What devices and browsers are supported? +

MAD Training Studio works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It is fully responsive on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
