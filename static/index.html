Your Interpretation Journey

Master each level to unlock the next. Start with Shadowing.

Shadowing
Repeat the speaker simultaneously in the same language. Builds listening, rhythm, and spoken fluency at broadcast pace.
Beginner Same language
Watch → Shadow simultaneously → Get fluency score
Consecutive
Listen to segments, take notes, then deliver your interpretation during the pause. Core conference skill.
Intermediate Cross-language
Watch segment → Pause → Interpret → Score
Escort/Liaison
Informal, bidirectional interpreting for business, social, or administrative settings. Emphasizes natural conversation and cultural mediation.
Intermediate Bidirectional, informal
Converse both ways → Score on cultural mediation & naturalness
Simultaneous
Interpret in real-time while the speaker talks. Maximum cognitive challenge — full conference booth standard.
Advanced Real-time
Watch + Interpret simultaneously → Analyze accuracy
Chuchotage
Whispered simultaneous, no booth or equipment, delivered at close proximity to 1-2 listeners. Adds volume discipline and noise resilience.
Advanced Real-time, whispered
Watch + Whisper-interpret live → Score on volume & noise control
Sight Translation
Written-input, oral-output interpreting. Read a document on screen and deliver an oral rendition at a sustained pace.
Advanced Written → Oral
Read document → Speak rendition → Score on register & WPM throughput
OPI / VRI
Over-the-Phone & Video Remote Interpreting. Triadic communication between two simulated parties — toggle video on for the VRI visual channel.
Professional Triadic
Listen to call scenario → Interpret both parties → Toggle video for VRI
Relay
Relay interpreting — pass through a pivot language between two interpreters who do not share a common language. Coming soon.
Expert Coming Soon
Interpreter A → Relay → Interpreter B
